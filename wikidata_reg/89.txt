originated in 1965 match with heavily influenced Lees about marital arts After about three minutes of combat, Wong Jack Man conceded Lee concluded that the fight had lasted too long and that he had failed to live up to his potential using his Wing Chun techniques He took the view that traditional martial arts techniques were too rigid and formalistic to be practical in scenarios of chaotic street fighting Lee decided to develop system with an emphasis on "practicality, flexibility, speed, and efficiency" He started to use different methods of training such as for strength, running for endurance, stretching for flexibility, and many others which he constantly adapted
